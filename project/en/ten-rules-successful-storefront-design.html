Store Front Design

The good design of the storefront plays an important role in the success and continuity of the project

The storefront is the customer's first impression because it is the first thing he looks at. If the front has a beautiful and attractive design, the customer will often feel curious about the things and products offered by the store and will enter.

Designed by Abdulrahman Majed

Information about storefront design:

The storefront plays an important role in determining the type of commercial activity for the store when designing the store’s decor. That is why most store owners emphasize the design of a beautiful storefront decorated with decorations to attract the largest number of customers. Not paying attention to the facade can make your store empty of customers and buyers. Even if you offer high quality products.

Store front design pictures

Basic steps for designing a storefront:

It is important to clad the walls surrounding the storefronts to give the store the desired and eye-catching elegance.

It is preferable that the facade be in a bright color so that the most demanded and discounted goods are displayed behind it, and the lighting is focused under and above these goods so that it is clear and attractive to passersby next to the shop.

  • - Decorating the exterior facade with attractive paintings, which is one of the foundations for the decoration of shops,

  • - Marking in a wide banner the opening and closing hours of the store,

  • - Focus the lighting firmly on the facade so that the store appears to stand out from a distance,

  • - Ensure the consistency of the colors used in facade and wall paints,

  • - Be sure to design a large front with a canopy, in order to protect products from damage due to sunlight.
