Information about the design of the villa

To design your villa and make it unique and have the conditions of comfort and safety and make it safer for your children and family, you must respect some of the basic points that we will mention to you.

Designed by Abdulrahman Majed

The main points of designing and building a villa:

  1. To respond to the changing needs of the inhabitants of the home,

  2. Easy navigation in and around the villa

  3. Easy access to things, furniture and basic design features,

  4. Making maximum use of natural lighting and sunlight to avoid moisture,

  5. Provide a green environment with the swimming pool if enough space is available for that,

  6. Design that meets the needs of the population, both current and future,

  7. Using the site well and not wasting land,

  8. Good design for the local climate to not depend on cooling or heating systems too much.
