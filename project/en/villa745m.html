Villa Design 745m²

Villa | 745 m²

With an area of ​​745 m, this villa has been designed in a completely new form and has a natural stone facade. It also has beautiful external lights.

Information about the design of the villa:

Every one of us has thought before or thinks of living in a villa with a beautiful design. It is one of the aspirations of homeowners, as the villas are elegant in the exterior and in the comfortable interior designs, making them a dream that many are trying to achieve.

If you intend to buy or build a new villa and move your life to the next stage, you should take a look at these wonderful architectural designs, to get an idea of ​​the most beautiful designs and to benefit from them in thinking about the design of your villa.

This luxurious design reflects a sophisticated look with a very large size and can accommodate a large number of individuals with many interior and exterior features.

This design is suitable for large families, as the villa is large in size and the number of interior rooms, as for its exterior design, it is largely made of natural stones.

Very simple design, but gorgeous inside and out, with an artistic touch of wood and natural stones.
