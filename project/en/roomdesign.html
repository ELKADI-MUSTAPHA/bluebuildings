Bedroom design information:

The bedroom is one of the most important and very personal matters where the person is cut off from the outside world to rest inside it between its walls in his own world, so we all agree that bedrooms should support us, contain and provide us with renewed positive energy so that we can continue life in peace, not Bedrooms have specific forms, one of which can be chosen and applied completely, because each room has its own privacy that differs from the other according to its shape, size, tastes and whims of its owners.

Bedroom Design Basics:

One of the basics of modern design for bedrooms is simplicity and the absence of unnecessary things in the room so that we can benefit from more free space after placing furniture and decorations, because the sense of space is what makes a person comfortable and happy, as well as for luxurious bedrooms and free space to add things Others in the future.

Modern bedroom designs are characterized by simple shapes of furniture and walls and decoration of accessories in common colors and clear and smooth lines without complications, and it is advisable to avoid a large number of furniture.

The modern bedroom depends mainly on natural lighting, such as large windows or walls made of glass bricks. It also has side lighting points in gypsum decorations. As for the curtains, they are designed to ensure privacy due to the sanctity and privacy of the bedroom, but at the same time, they allow the largest amount of sunlight to enter the room.

Design the walls with a moderate graphic pattern in light and soft colors, such as white, two shades of beige or light blue, and that the number of colors does not exceed 3 in the entire room, because colors affect us in a negative or positive way, either we feel cold And fun or heat and sadness.

When designing the room, one must take into account the person's use inside the room and provide his personal needs, such as a corner for watching TV, a corner for the office, computer and reading, in addition to the bathroom if the space is sufficient and spacious, as a reminder, these personal things differ from one person to another according to interests and job Everyone.

Bedroom Bathroom Design

If the area of the room is large, then adding a bathroom attached to the room is one of the signs of luxury and luxury for the bedroom and guarantees you a lot of privacy and comfort. There are various designs for bathrooms, such as classic designs and modern designs, so care must be taken to choose a bathroom design consistent with the bedroom to achieve Balance and harmony in the room.

Bedroom bathroom design steps:

  • Choose the bathroom so that it is practical and simple and contains only the necessary things,

  • Measure the available space to determine the pieces to be installed,

  • The design of the bathroom with a suitable size for the space of the room,

  • The colors of the bathroom match the colors of the room,

  • The arrangement of the bathroom makes it beautiful and special,

  • Choose rough tiles for durability, durability and non-slip wear,

  • Distribution of lighting in a coordinated and beautiful way.
