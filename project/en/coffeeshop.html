Design Coffee Shop

There is no doubt that the most widespread and necessary facilities in our time are cafes and restaurants, due to their important role in entertainment, meetings, so the comfortable and attractive design is one of the basics of this project.

Information about the design of the coffee shop:

The exterior design of the cafés gives a distinctive character and an aesthetic appearance to draw the attention of customers, and therefore the exterior design must be distinguished by sophistication, creativity and elegant shape, but this exterior design needs great attention to the interior space of the cafés.

The basic steps for designing a successful coffee shop:

  • Choose the type of cafe "individuals - families - women - individuals and families" (To determine the design and implementation plan)

  • Determine the distribution of lighting places to include the cafe from all sides,

  • Take care of the smooth flow of traffic inside the café,

  • Easy access to parking,

  • Pay attention to decorations, wall paintings and colors, as they are the first thing that customers notice when they enter the café.

Methods of designing a coffee shop or café:

After determining the type of café, reviewing it and studying it in terms of space and location, the next step is to design. After knowing the client's philosophy, vision, special orientations, and the expected total cost, we will mention some basic steps:

  • - Locale design and layout,

  • - Focusing on the main points in terms of the distribution of spaces for sessions, the operating area
    (the counter) and the traffic inside the café,

  • - Designing outlets and lighting distribution points in all areas of the café,

  • - 3D design that gives a realistic final image, represented in colors, floors, walls and interior decorations,

  • - Designing the method of distributing sanitary and electrical equipment and installations.
