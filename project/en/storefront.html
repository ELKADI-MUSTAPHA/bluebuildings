Store front design

The storefront is the customer's first impression because it is the first thing he looks at, and if the interface is poorly designed, most likely the customer will avoid entering the store.

Information about storefront design:

It is important that the walls surrounding the storefronts be covered to give the store the desired and eye-catching elegance. It can also be covered with large black marble or black stone, or to avoid the high cost, paint can be used so that a suitable paint is selected for the store.

It is preferable that the facade be in a bright color so that the most demanded and discounted goods are displayed behind it, and the lighting is focused under and above these goods so that they are clear and attractive to passers-by next to the shop.

Designing a commercial store to be suitable for most types of merchandise by distributing lighting inside the store to illuminate the entire store and placing some lights towards the discount merchandise display area. Lighting and distributing lights professionally are among the most important basics in the interior decoration of shops.
