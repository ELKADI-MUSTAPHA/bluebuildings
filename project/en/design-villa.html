Some types of designs for villas:

  1. Modern design:

    Doing away with complex geometric shapes and relying on simple geometric shapes that show the beauty and elegance of the place, The design of the wide facades with glass to enjoy the natural landscape and take advantage of the natural lighting, Abandoning the division of interior spaces with walls and focusing on creating an open space to give a sense of comfort and spaciousness, and using modern cladding materials such as wood, stone panels, facade porcelain and other materials that give aesthetics to a building.

  2. Classic design:

    This design is based on the principle of symmetry in the form, where the right and left of the entrance are the same, with the entrances magnified and columns added to the sides of the entrance.

  3. Modern Classic Design:

    This design is based on the usual luxury and prestige of classic designs, with huge entrances in simple shapes, with the addition of modern design touches such as the use of modern materials.
