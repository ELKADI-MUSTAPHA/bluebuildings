Design Commercial Building

Commercial buildings have become characterized by the beauty of their unique and architectural design, where shop owners and companies are requesting modern engineering and architectural designs that suit their field of work and trade. Therefore, Blue Buildings is interested in designing commercial buildings and storefronts that are modern and suitable for every commercial activity.

Design and build a commercial project:

The centers, commercial buildings, and malls are among the ideal places to enjoy a wonderful shopping experience in Saudi Arabia. And building a commercial project: a mall, a mall, or a commercial building.

Information about the design of a mall:

The mall is characterized by the multiplicity of services it provides, because it consists of several main centers, including shops, restaurants, a coffee shop, a car park, a women's spa and many other centers that serve many people at the same time, where they resort to because of the atmosphere it provides. Suitable for shopping, due to the presence of air conditioners and the provision of many services that facilitate the shopping process.

The exterior design of the commercial building gives a distinctive character and an aesthetic appearance to draw the attention of customers, and therefore the exterior design must be distinguished by sophistication, creativity and elegant shape, but this exterior design needs great attention to the interior space of the commercial building.

When the design of your project takes into account and meets the requirements of the times, it helps you to develop your project and deliver it to the largest possible number of target customers, and saves you a lot of time and money.

Through design, you give an identity and personality to your project and increase customer confidence in the services you provide. Paying attention to the design of the commercial identity contributes to the success of your project by clarifying your goals, objectives and the services provided by your project.

Basic steps for designing a mall:

  • The project should be located in a place away from noise,

  • It is preferable to use artificial lighting,

  • use of soundproofing,

  • A car park capable of carrying a large and suitable number of cars,

  • 25% of the total area of ​​the mall is designated for stairs and corridors,

  • Respecting the green spaces inside and outside the mall,

  • Attention to the distribution of lighting to include the mall from all sides,

  • Take care of the smooth flow of traffic inside the mall,

  • Easy access to items for sale.
