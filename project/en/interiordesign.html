Interior Design

Interior design pays attention to the interior details of buildings such as decoration, colors and lighting, transforming empty spaces into a place to live or work.

Interior design information:

The interior design of the office, home or workplace directly affects the psychology of people. Either you feel comfortable and happy when you enter, or you feel uncomfortable and uncomfortable. The interior design of the house makes it more organized, because everything has its own place and makes us reassured. And it prevents the desire to change the look or arrangement of the house every day.

Many people want to change the interior design of the house or when building a new house, so they need ideas that suit their taste and personality, fortunately Blue Buildings offers all interior design services for all types of projects.

Interior Design Basics:

  • The appropriate choice of colors that distinguish and express the place,

  • Attention to the distribution of lighting because it is one of the most important elements of interior design,

  • Following simplicity in design and choosing furniture and decorative pieces,

  • Record space dimensions, locate doors, windows, and sources of natural light,

  • The balance in the composition, where the viewer feels comfortable watching the overall shape of the design.
