Villa Design 800m²

Are you thinking of designing your own villa?

Blue Buildings saves you searching for a designer. We offer you an exterior and interior design service for villas and all types of homes, whether modern, classic or neoclassical. We use specialized design programs by the most skilled designers and offer you everything new in the field of exterior and interior design.

Designed by Abdulrahman Majed

The main points of designing and building a villa:

  1. To respond to the changing needs of the inhabitants of the home,

  2. Easy navigation in and around the villa

  3. Easy access to things, furniture and basic design features,

  4. Making maximum use of natural lighting and sunlight to avoid moisture,

  5. Provide a green environment with the swimming pool if enough space is available for that,

  6. Design that meets the needs of the population, both current and future,

  7. Using the site well and not wasting land,

  8. Good design for the local climate to not depend on cooling or heating systems too much.

Villa Design | 800 m²

This western-style villa is very suitable in our Arab countries, as it gives us the same characteristics found in Arab villas, from the presence of open corridors, to windows and spacious balconies.

The front facade is completely based on a lot of glass facades and columns, and there is a very wide entrance to give the villa a royal look.

This design is suitable for large families, as the villa is large in size and the number of interior rooms, as for its exterior design, it is largely made of natural stones.

Very simple design, but gorgeous inside and out, with an artistic touch of wood and natural stones.
